Ev-Charger Ac/Dc

Electrical Charger Ac-Dc

Project Description

  • The off-board EV charger, based on a digital half-bridge LLC topology, uses a mathematical model to achieve high energy density while increasing safety, reducing weight, and improving efficiency in compliance with AIS-156 standards.
  • It supports multiple battery types, and the available charging methods include pre-charge, constant current (CC), constant voltage (CV), constant power (CP), floating/trickle charging, and power exchange via CAN command.
